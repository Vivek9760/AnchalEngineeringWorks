About Anchal
Engineering Works

Anchal Engineering Works is a trusted industrial services provider based in Roorkee, Haridwar, Uttarakhand. We specialise in CNC laser cutting, NC bending, power press, sheet shearing, lathe machining, and fabrication services.

Our facility is equipped with modern CNC laser cutting machines, NC press brakes, power presses (5T–50T), shearing machines, and lathe machines — enabling us to deliver high-quality components with speed and consistency.
